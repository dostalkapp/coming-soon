Privacy is in our code.

Your privacy is not an option; it's the standard. We believe your data should belong only to you.

End-to-End Encryption

Hype uses the **Signal Protocol** for all personal messaging. When you send a message, it’s scrambled into an unbreakable code that can only be unlocked by the recipient’s device. No one else has the keys—including us.

Zero Knowledge

We don’t store metadata. We don’t track who you talk to, when you talk to them, or what your location is. You are a ghost in our system.

Open Source Foundation

Our encryption libraries are open for public audit. Anyone can verify that we do exactly what we say we do.

Biometric Protection

Layer your security with FaceID or TouchID. Even if someone has your phone, they don't have your conversations.

Anti-Spam Shield

AI-powered filters stop scammers and bots before they reach your inbox, without ever reading your private content.

We don't sell your data.
We don't even have it.

Unlike other platforms, Hype is funded by users, not advertisers. This ensures our interests are always aligned with your privacy.
